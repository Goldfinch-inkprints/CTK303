Women in Technology's goal is to promote interest and enrollment of women in into technology-based majors and careers. We want to improve the professional skills of our fellow associates in technology to further the support of those who are underrepresented in the technology-based fields.

At WIT, we have speakers come to talk about their companies, how to build a career. and the impact that women have in technology. We also have nights where we have resume and cover letter workshops, or talk about our classes. Once a month, we have a social nights where we play games or watch movies to get to know each other better.

Women in Technology is all about building that community around supporting our fellow Women in Technology. By supporting Women in Technology, we are all doing our part to build and support the future of an equal-gender represented Technology career field.
